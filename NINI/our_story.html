Last year has been challenging but it is the year that brought me the biggest blessing of my life, being your wife. We learnt a lot about each other , All those fights over the phone ,laughs ,the movies we'd watch on the phone together in Somalia. Our question nights that would end in fights, LOL. We would speak for hours when you were in Somalia and that got me so attached to you. You became my favorite person so quick I loved talking to you so much. Then you came to see me, God I was so nervous but it was so beautiful, I felt so safe with you it was crazy. Then you left to the USA to make everything a reality for us. You still made time for us and reassured that everything would work. You went above and beyond. And Im so grateful. You came with your family,Did a beautiful wedding, Bought me my dream ring and had an amazing honeymoon. We're almost married 4 months now and it feels amazing being your wife. Cant wait to spend the rest of our lives together.
